System requirements

Getting Started ››

It's hard to say the exact requirements.

The App works on ati 4670, but with strange graphic glitches, your mileage may vary.

I think it will work fine on ati 4700 series and above just fine.

It does work fine on ati 5450, and likely will on all ati 5000 series GPUs.

I think the same era nVidia's will show the same results, other than weird bugs.

The App was developed on AMD A10-5700 with ati 7660D GPU (384 shaders).

I would say, if the machine is a single core CPU, don't bother trying it.
